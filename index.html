WPI is proud to host the Women in Data Science Conference in partnership with Stanford University. This one-day regional event will showcase local talent, bringing together individuals from industry and academia to celebrate the contributions of Women in Data Science.

Program

The Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference aims to inspire and educate data scientists worldwide, regardless of gender, and support women in the field. This annual conference is held at Stanford University and 100+ locations worldwide via livestream or delayed broadcast.


The Central Massachusetts Conference program will include:

  • Live-stream content from the Global Women in Data Science Conference at Stanford University , including technical and keynote talks by leading women at top companies and universities.
  • In-person talks from local women speakers.
  • A panel discussion on Data Science careers in academia vs industry.
  • A poster session to showcase research by students.
  • Opportunities to network with local researchers, industry professionals, and academic leaders.


Detailed agenda items and speakers will be posted mid-January.

The event is free and open to all members of the public. All genders are invited to participate in the conference, which will feature exclusively female speakers and presenters.
