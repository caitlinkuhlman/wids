The Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference aims to inspire and educate data scientists worldwide, regardless of gender, and support women in the field. This annual conference is held at Stanford University and 100+ locations worldwide via livestream or delayed broadcast.


WPI is proud to host the Women in Data Science Conference in partnership with Stanford University. This one-day regional event for Central Massachusetts will showcase local talent, bringing together individuals from industry and academia to celebrate the contributions of Women in Data Science.
